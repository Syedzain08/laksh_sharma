Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 March 2026

Laksh Sharma is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle data when you visit www.lakshsharma.co.uk.

What We Collect

We do not collect any personal information through this website. There are no contact forms, newsletter sign-ups, or payment systems.

We use Umami Analytics, a privacy-focused analytics tool, to understand how visitors use the site. Umami collects:

  • Pages visited and time spent on pages
  • Referring websites and search terms
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Country or region (derived from IP address, which is not stored)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

Important: Umami does not use cookies, does not collect personal information, and cannot track you across other websites. Your IP address is used only to determine your general location at country level and is not stored. Umami is hosted within the EU.

Direct Contact

If you contact Laksh directly by phone or email, any personal data you provide (such as your name, phone number, or event details) will be used solely to respond to your enquiry and arrange bookings. This data is not shared with third parties and is retained only as long as necessary.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies. Umami Analytics is a cookieless solution that stores nothing on your device. No consent banner is required.

Legal Basis for Processing

Under UK GDPR, anonymous analytics data is processed on the basis of legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f)) — specifically, understanding how the website is used in order to improve it. This processing is minimal, anonymous, and poses no risk to your privacy.

If you contact Laksh directly, personal data is processed on the basis of your consent and/or to take steps at your request prior to entering a contract (Article 6(1)(a) and 6(1)(b)).

Data Retention

Anonymous analytics data is retained for 24 months before automatic deletion. Personal data shared via direct contact is retained only as long as needed to fulfil your enquiry or booking.

Your Rights Under UK GDPR

Because this website only processes anonymous, non-personal data through analytics, most GDPR rights do not apply to site analytics. However, you have the right to:

  • Opt out of analytics by disabling JavaScript in your browser
  • Object to anonymous analytics collection by contacting us

If you have shared personal data by contacting Laksh directly, you have full UK GDPR rights, including the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, data portability, and object to processing.

Third-Party Links

This website may link to external platforms (such as Instagram, YouTube, or TikTok). Please review the privacy policies of those platforms when you visit them, as we are not responsible for their data practices.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
